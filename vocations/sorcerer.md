# Sorcerer

[!badge variant="danger" text="Magic"] [!badge variant="warning" text="DPS"]

Powerful magic user specializing in destructive spells and area-of-effect damage.

## Strengths

* Highest spell damage output
* Devastating offensive magic
* Strong AOE spells
* Energy & fire specialization
* High burst damage

## Weaknesses

* Lowest HP (most fragile)
* Requires careful mana management
* Must stay at range
* Equipment-dependent
* Dies quickly if caught

## Equipment

* **Weapons:** Wands, rods
* **Armor:** Light armor/robes (magic bonuses)
* **Focus:** Magic level, spell damage, mana
* **Accessories:** Magic power boosters

## Key Spells

* Exori Vis - Energy attack
* Exori Gran Vis - Strong energy attack
* Exevo Gran Vis Lux - Massive energy burst
* Exori Flam - Fire attack
* Exevo Gran Mas Flam - Area fire attack
* Exura - Basic healing (limited)

!!!danger Sorcerer
Most fragile vocation. Keep distance from enemies. High damage = low survivability.
!!!

!!!tip Sorcerer
Focus on magic level and spell damage. Kill enemies before they reach you. Always carry mana potions.
!!!
