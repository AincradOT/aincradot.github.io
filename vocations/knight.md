# Knight

[!badge variant="primary" text="Melee"] [!badge variant="info" text="Tank"]

Frontline warrior specializing in melee combat with high HP and physical damage.

## Strengths

* Highest HP pool
* Strong defense and armor
* Powerful melee attacks (swords, axes, clubs)
* Can tank and hold aggro
* Sustained combat endurance

## Weaknesses

* Melee range only
* Lower magic resistance
* Slower movement
* Mana-dependent abilities

## Equipment

* **Weapons:** Swords, axes, clubs
* **Armor:** Heavy plate armor
* **Shields:** Essential for blocking
* **Focus:** Defense, HP, physical attack

## Key Spells

* Exori - Basic melee attack
* Exori Gran - Strong melee attack
* Exori Mas - Area melee attack
* Utito Tempo - Berserker mode (damage boost)
* Utani Hur - Speed boost

!!!tip Knight
Prioritize HP and defense. Your job is to survive and protect the party, not deal the most damage.
!!!
