# Paladin

[!badge variant="success" text="Ranged"] [!badge variant="info" text="Versatile"]

Balanced vocation that excels at ranged combat with both physical and magical damage.

## Strengths

* Versatile (ranged and melee capable)
* Good survivability
* Self-healing abilities
* Effective solo play
* Both physical and magic damage

## Weaknesses

* Jack of all trades, master of none
* Lower damage than specialized DPS
* Requires arrows/bolts
* Needs both physical and magic equipment

## Equipment

* **Weapons:** Bows, crossbows, spears
* **Armor:** Medium armor
* **Ammunition:** Arrows or bolts
* **Focus:** Balanced stats, distance fighting

## Key Spells

* Exori San - Holy melee attack
* Exevo Mas San - Area holy attack
* Exura - Self-healing
* Exura Gran - Strong self-healing
* Utani Hur - Speed boost

!!! tip "Paladin Tip"
Master kiting - attack from range while moving. Keep arrows/bolts stocked and manage mana for healing.
!!!
