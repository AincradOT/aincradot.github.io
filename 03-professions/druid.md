# Druid

[!badge variant="success" text="Support"] [!badge variant="info" text="Healer"]

Support vocation specializing in healing, utility spells, and nature-based magic.

## Strengths

* Best healing spells
* Support buffs and utility
* Ice & earth offensive magic
* Essential for group content
* Can heal, buff, and deal damage

## Weaknesses

* Lower damage than Sorcerers
* Most effective in groups
* Mana-intensive healing
* Party survival depends on you
* Less effective solo

## Equipment

* **Weapons:** Wands, rods
* **Armor:** Light to medium (magic bonuses)
* **Focus:** Magic level, healing power, mana
* **Accessories:** Healing and support boosters

## Key Spells

* Exura - Basic healing
* Exura Gran - Strong healing
* Exura Vita - Massive healing
* Exura Mas - Area healing
* Exori Frigo - Ice attack
* Exori Tera - Earth attack
* Exevo Mas Frigo - Area ice attack
* Utani Hur - Speed boost

!!! tip "Druid Tip"
Keep your party alive first. You can deal damage when safe, but healing is priority. Monitor party HP and mana.
!!!

!!! note "Party Essential"
Druids are always in demand for group content. Your healing makes you highly valuable.
!!!
